The Story

Starting from the humble beginnings of this semester, I come pretty far since this semester has started especially in regards of coding and the other skills that were required to complete the COMP1917 course.

Throughout module 0, I have taken heaps of new information that has helped me progress through this module such as the operations of the microprocessors which is relatively new to me. Prior to this module, I have never knew how a microprocessor worked and the concept of the microprocessor. Also in this module, I learnt that solving problems doesn't always have to involve solving the problem and that by different levels of abstraction that it will become easier to solve the problem.

This module has given me the opportunity to attempt many interesting tasks such as toast activity and the coding of the microprocessor in order to print different stuff.

On the completion of this module, I found that I discovered many new methods of understanding and thinking not just simply more knowledge. Especially in the 4001 and 4002 activities, I found myself using abstraction to find the simpler solution since it seemed my complex thinking had left me on a point where the activity was beyond my reach only to find that it was way simpler than I thought. It is now that I find using abstraction and basic thinking is more helpful in problem solving rather than finding solutions outside the box first.
